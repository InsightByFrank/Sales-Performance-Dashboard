## 🚀 Overview  
The **Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive analytics solution designed to provide deep insights into sales performance, revenue trends, and customer behavior.  
It enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify patterns, and make data-driven decisions with ease.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Track overall sales performance across time  
- Compare current performance with previous periods  
- Analyze customer segments and purchasing behavior  
- Identify high-performing products and revenue drivers  

---

## 📌 Key Features  

### 📈 Revenue Analysis  
- Total Revenue tracking with percentage growth  
- Monthly revenue trend visualization  
- Comparison between current and previous month revenue  

### 🛒 Sales Metrics  
- Total Units Sold  
- Total Orders  
- Average Order Value (AOV)  

### 📊 Trend Insights  
- Revenue trend line across months  
- Identification of peak and low-performing periods  

### 👥 Customer Insights  
- Revenue distribution by **age group**  
- Revenue breakdown by **gender**  

### 📦 Product Performance  
- Revenue contribution by product category  
- Quick identification of top-performing products  

### 🔍 Advanced Analysis  
- Relationship between **quantity sold and revenue**  
- Helps uncover sales patterns and performance drivers  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  

- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculations and measures  
- **Excel / CSV** – Data source and preprocessing

## 📊 Key Insights  

- Revenue shows fluctuations across months with identifiable peaks  
- Certain customer segments contribute significantly more to revenue  
- Product categories vary in performance, highlighting optimization opportunities  
- Sales volume does not always directly translate to higher revenue

## 💡 Business Value  

This dashboard empowers decision-makers to:
- Monitor performance in real time  
- Identify growth opportunities and risks  
- Understand customer behavior and segmentation  
- Improve product and pricing strategies  
- Make informed, data-driven business decisions
